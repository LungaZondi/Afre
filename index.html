Manifesto

“Beyond” isn't a size, or a price. It's what happens when a sleeve refuses to stop at the wrist.

Afré Lumière begins with a simple complaint: most clothing asks the body to behave. Hems stop where convention says they should. Sleeves end at the wrist, because that's where sleeves end. We started by asking what happens if they didn't.

Every study in this collection — the sash, the silhouette, the train — is an argument for a little more room. Not louder clothing. Quieter clothing that simply declines to stay inside the lines it was given.

The process

Every piece begins on paper.

Before fabric, before a single pin, there's a page in a notebook. Five studies — in pencil, then ink — are kept exactly as they were drawn, alongside the thinking that shaped each one.
